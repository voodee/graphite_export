Warranty and assistance
We don’t expect you’ll have any mechanical problems with your new Triumph motorcycle, but having a 2-year unlimited mileage warranty really offers extra peace of mind. In addition, Triumph Assist, developed in association with the RAC, is a roadside recovery package that you can rely on if the unexpected happens, and our free Safety Recall Checker is perfect for discovering if you’re eligible for free replacement of any components.
Bike care
There are plenty of ways you might enhance your Triumph ride. As well as having reliable roadside recovery in place, implementing sensible security precautions, choosing the best possible accessories, fitting the right tyres, and using the correct oil will all significantly extend your motorcycle’s performance.
